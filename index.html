Indian National Movment Project

This project is an initative to expand the knowledge about people involved in the national movement going beyond the traditional medium of books and magazines. Explore Delhi's roads commemorated after people involved in the national movement to learn about India's independence struggle.

Click the map to discover roads named after people involved in the national movement and explore archival books, journals and gallery. Don't forget to see the about section to learn about the massacres, movements, protest, violences that took place.
